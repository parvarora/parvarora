## 🌟 The story behind ShareIn

While travelling, eating, or scrolling — have you ever come across a job post you couldn't apply to *right now* (resume needs a tweak), so you save it on LinkedIn… and then forget you ever saved it?

I missed plenty of opportunities exactly that way. **So I built ShareIn — the most personal app on my phone.** It collects every job I save from **any** platform (LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Glassdoor) into one place, and **reminds me before the deadline**. When I showed friends the prototype, the reaction was instant: *“bro, I’ve needed this for so long.”*

I obsessed over removing friction — it takes just **2 extra clicks** to save any job into ShareIn.

---

## ✨ What it does

- **📥 Save from any app** — ShareIn appears right in your phone’s share tray (just like WhatsApp or Instagram). Tap **Share → ShareIn** from any job post.
- **🔗 Smart auto-title** — a custom algorithm extracts a relevant job title straight from the URL — no heavy AI backend needed.
- **⏰ Deadline tracking** — set a deadline (default **7 days**), add notes (e.g. *“update resume”*), and save.
- **🔔 Deadline reminders** — its superpower: it pings you before a deadline so you never miss an application again.
- **🗂️ One unified dashboard** — every saved job, across every platform, in a single scrollable view with urgency indicators.
- **🖥️ Works on laptop too** — open the same link on your laptop, type `s` + Enter, and you’re at your saved jobs in seconds.
- **🌍 Truly universal** — because it keys off the URL, it works everywhere — your personal job collector.

## 📖 How to use ShareIn

### 1. Install (Android · Chrome)
Open **[sharein-917a1.web.app](https://sharein-917a1.web.app/login)** in **Chrome**, sign in with Google, and install it as an app (Add to Home Screen). It’s a **Progressive Web App** — no Play Store needed. 👉 **Enable notifications** to unlock the reminders.

> 🎥 New here? [Watch the short demo](https://lnkd.in/g-CKWXgU) to see install + usage end-to-end.

### 2. Save a job from anywhere
On a job post (LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, Glassdoor…), tap **Send / Share → ShareIn**. The capture page opens with the **URL pre-filled** and a **title generated automatically**.

### 3. Set deadline + notes (2 clicks)
Pick a **deadline** (defaults to 7 days), add an optional **note** like *“tailor resume for this one,”* and hit **Save**. Done.

### 4. Apply on your own time
At the end of the day, open ShareIn on your laptop, jump to your saved post in seconds, and apply — and let the **reminders** make sure nothing slips.

## 🧠 The design thinking behind it

The clever part isn’t a big AI backend — it’s deciding *not* to build one:

1. **First idea:** share the post to a LinkedIn chat and scrape details from there → ❌ LinkedIn doesn’t allow it.
2. **Second idea:** share to an app and have an AI extract title + deadline → over-engineered for a task the user can do in 2 clicks.
3. **Insight:** the **URL is the trump card.** I wrote an algorithm that extracts a *good-enough* title straight from the URL, and let the user set the deadline in 2 clicks.
4. **Result:** a lightweight **PWA**, no complex AI backend, that’s **universal** across every job platform.

> It’s not a world-changing idea — but I use it *every single day*, and it genuinely made my life easier. That’s the point.
